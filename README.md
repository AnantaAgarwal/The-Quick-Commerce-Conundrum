## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the operational performance of a fictional e-commerce platform **QuickBazaar** using customer, delivery, and inventory data.  
The objective is to identify business problems, extract meaningful insights, and propose data-driven solutions using analytics and visualization tools.

The project was completed as part of an academic mini-project and emphasizes **practical business insights over complex modeling**.

---

## Project Objectives
- Analyze **customer behavior** using order value and rating data  
- Evaluate **delivery performance** and identify delay hotspots  
- Study **inventory stockout patterns** across product categories  
- Build **interactive dashboards** for clear communication of insights  
- Provide **business recommendations** based on data analysis

## Key Analysis Performed
- AOV trend analysis over time  
- Customer rating distribution analysis  
- Identification of delivery delay hotspots  
- Correlation between delivery time and customer ratings  
- Inventory stockout pattern analysis  

---

## Key Insights
- Delivery delays in specific pincodes significantly impact customer satisfaction  
- Higher transit time correlates with lower customer ratings  
- Certain product categories face frequent stockouts, indicating demand forecasting issues  
- Stable AOV trends suggest consistent customer spending behavior  

---

## Business Recommendations
- Optimize delivery routes and allocate more riders to high-delay areas  
- Improve dispatch efficiency to reduce transit time  
- Introduce buffer stock for high-demand categories  
- Use historical data for better inventory forecasting  
- Improve customer communication during delays  

---

The project demonstrates how **data analytics can be used to identify operational inefficiencies and support better business decision-making**.  
It highlights the importance of dashboards and visual storytelling in presenting analytical findings effectively.
